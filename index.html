Backlog

Backlog is an app designed to help you keep track of your growing list of unfinished games.

List Format

Quickly refer to the games in your backlog with the handy list on the main page.

Jog Your Memory

Save a description of your games, and view a related web page to refresh your memory. This way, you won't forget where you were, no matter how long it's been since you last played!

Authors and Contributors
